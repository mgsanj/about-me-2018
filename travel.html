The souvenirs we usually buy are... magnets. Yes, I know, it doesn't sound very interesting, but we actually have a total of ... 63 magnets! Yes, we are a magnet LOVING family!

Another example is, in Japan, you can see many famous temples. We walked around these temples, admiring their art, and also ate LOTS OF FOOD.
